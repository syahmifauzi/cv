Profile

My name is Syahmi Fauzi. I'm a front-end web developer based in Malaysia. When I was 16 years old, I join D-Code Camp in Kuching, Sarawak as a participant. That was the first time I meet HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Then, I continue being a self-taught web developer and now I can make from small to medium size of websites.

When It comes to frontend web development, I really like working with preprocessor like Pug and Sass and a building tool like GulpJS because they help make the development faster.

In university, I participate in coding and programming competition. This kind of competition teach me how to solve real world problems. It's tough sometimes but fun to learnt especially when you have friends who are as passion as you.

I'm a kind of people who loves building things on the Internet and share it with others. Github is my favourite place to host all of those things.
